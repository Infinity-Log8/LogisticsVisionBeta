# Data / Performance Marketer (Optional, Phase 2)

**Reports to:** Strategic Marketer
**Function:** Marketing
**Note:** Optional in phase 1. Often a fractional contractor before going full-time.

## Core Responsibilities
- Own paid acquisition: search, social, retargeting, partnerships
- Build and maintain marketing attribution and reporting
- Run growth experiments — test, measure, iterate, kill or scale
- Manage marketing analytics stack and data pipelines
- Translate funnel data into product and content recommendations

## AI-Augmented Responsibilities
- **AI handles:** analyzing campaign performance across channels, drafting ad copy
  variations for testing, summarizing weekly performance reports, generating SQL
  queries against the marketing data warehouse, surfacing attribution anomalies
- **Human judges:** which channels actually scale vs. which look good in week one,
  when an AI-generated insight is correlation not causation, when to spend more vs.
  reallocate, what an unusual data point actually means

## Key Skills to Hire For
- Has owned a paid budget of $500K+ annually and improved its efficiency
- Strong SQL and at least one BI tool (Looker, Mode, Hex, Metabase)
- Comfortable with statistical significance and experiment design
- Skeptical of attribution — knows where last-click breaks down
- Comfortable using AI for analysis acceleration without trusting it blindly

## Interview Signals (AI-native vs. traditional)
- **Strong:** Can describe an experiment they ran that returned a negative result and
  what they did with it
- **Strong:** Has used AI to draft 20 ad variations and can describe how they picked
  the winning batch
- **Strong:** Has a calibrated view on where attribution lies
- **Weak:** Reports last-click ROAS as gospel
- **Weak:** Treats AI as either a writer-replacement or an analyst-replacement

## Compensation Band (US, mid-range senior IC)
- Base: $150K - $185K
- Equity: 0.15% - 0.4% (early-stage)
- Total comp target: $200K - $260K
- Fractional rate: $175 - $250/hour

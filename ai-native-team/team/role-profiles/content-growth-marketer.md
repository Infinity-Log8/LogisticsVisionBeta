# Content / Growth Marketer

**Reports to:** Strategic Marketer
**Function:** Marketing

## Core Responsibilities
- Produce all customer-facing content: blog posts, docs, emails, social, video scripts,
  landing pages
- Own the editorial calendar and brand voice in writing
- Manage SEO, lifecycle marketing, and community channels
- Test and iterate on landing pages, headlines, and CTAs
- Act as editor-in-chief of all AI-drafted content — last line of brand defense

## AI-Augmented Responsibilities
- **AI handles:** drafting blog posts from outlines, generating 10 headline variations,
  repurposing one piece across formats (blog → tweet thread → newsletter → video
  script), SEO keyword clustering, drafting email sequences from a brief
- **Human judges:** brand voice, factual accuracy, when an AI draft is fluent-but-empty,
  what story actually lands with the audience, when to throw out the AI draft and
  start over

## Key Skills to Hire For
- High-volume publishing track record — has shipped 50+ pieces of strong work, not
  just polished a handful
- Distinctive written voice — can write something a reader recognizes as theirs
- Strong editorial judgment — can cut their own work without grief
- Comfortable with SEO and analytics but not slave to them
- Treats AI like a junior writer they're editing — useful, fallible, never the byline

## Interview Signals (AI-native vs. traditional)
- **Strong:** Brings a piece they wrote with AI and the same piece they wrote without,
  and can explain the editing process
- **Strong:** Has a personal point of view on a topic in your category — has been
  publishing for fun, not just for jobs
- **Strong:** Can rewrite a sample of corporate-speak into something a human would read
  in under 10 minutes
- **Weak:** Portfolio is heavy on social engagement metrics, light on actual writing
- **Weak:** Treats AI as a content-volume machine without editorial filtering

## Compensation Band (US, mid-range senior IC)
- Base: $130K - $170K
- Equity: 0.15% - 0.4% (early-stage)
- Total comp target: $170K - $220K

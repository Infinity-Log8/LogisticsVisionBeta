# Strategic Marketer

**Reports to:** Founder / CEO
**Function:** Marketing
**Note:** First marketing hire. Establishes positioning before there's anything to launch.

## Core Responsibilities
- Own product positioning, messaging hierarchy, and category narrative
- Run competitive intelligence — track competitors, market signals, customer perception
- Set the marketing strategy and channel mix
- Partner with PM and Design on launch strategy for every feature
- Own the customer voice in internal discussions — bring real quotes to roadmap debates

## AI-Augmented Responsibilities
- **AI handles:** competitor teardowns from public materials, scraping and synthesizing
  G2/Reddit/HN sentiment, drafting positioning statements in 5 variations, generating
  message-testing surveys, summarizing analyst notes and industry reports
- **Human judges:** which positioning actually differentiates vs. which sounds generic,
  what the market is ready to hear, when to lead vs. follow on category framing, what
  resonates with real customers vs. what looks good on a slide

## Key Skills to Hire For
- Has positioned at least one product successfully in a competitive category
- Writes crisp, opinionated copy — can rewrite a 200-word paragraph into 30 words
  without losing meaning
- Comfortable holding the line on brand voice against louder voices
- Quantitative literacy — can read funnel data and challenge a content team's metrics
- Strong taste in B2B and consumer copy

## Interview Signals (AI-native vs. traditional)
- **Strong:** Can show three pieces of positioning work and explain what got cut and why
- **Strong:** Has used AI to do a competitive teardown and can point to the part that
  needed human judgment to be useful
- **Strong:** Has changed a company's positioning and can describe the before/after
  on a metric that matters
- **Strong:** Has fired bad copy — knows what doesn't work and why
- **Weak:** Positioning sounds like every other category leader
- **Weak:** Defaults to demand-gen tactics before strategy is settled

## Compensation Band (US, mid-range senior IC)
- Base: $180K - $220K
- Equity: 0.3% - 0.8% (early-stage)
- Total comp target: $250K - $340K

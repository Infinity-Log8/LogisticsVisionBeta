# Senior Product Designer

**Reports to:** Founder / CEO (dotted line to Product Manager)
**Function:** Design

## Core Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end product UX from problem framing to shipped flows
- Build and maintain the design system; keep it lean and well-documented
- Run rapid prototyping cycles — sketches to clickable in days, not weeks
- Drive accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA minimum) into every component
- Partner with engineering on implementation fidelity and edge cases

## AI-Augmented Responsibilities
- **AI handles:** generating layout variations, populating components with realistic
  content, drafting accessibility audits, summarizing user interview transcripts,
  producing first-pass copy for empty states and microcopy
- **Human judges:** which variation actually solves the problem, whether the flow feels
  right, when "good enough" is a trap, where the design system needs a real new
  primitive vs. a clever composition

## Key Skills to Hire For
- Editing and synthesis over raw production speed
- Strong written communication — can write a design rationale in five clear sentences
- Systems thinking — understands trade-offs between flexibility and consistency
- Comfortable with code (HTML/CSS, Tailwind, basic React) — can prototype in-browser
- Has shipped at least one product end-to-end as the sole designer

## Interview Signals (AI-native vs. traditional)
- **Strong:** Walks through a design problem and shows three AI-generated directions,
  then explains why they kept one element from each — demonstrates editor mindset
- **Strong:** Has a personal prompt library or a strong opinion on when AI helps vs. hurts
- **Strong:** Can show a recent project where the design system caught a mistake
  before it shipped
- **Weak:** Treats AI as a threat or refuses to use it; or treats AI as a magic box
  and ships the first generation
- **Weak:** Portfolio is gorgeous mocks but no shipped product, or no opinion on
  accessibility

## Compensation Band (US, mid-range senior IC)
- Base: $165K - $200K
- Equity: 0.2% - 0.5% (early-stage) / RSUs at later stage
- Total comp target: $220K - $280K

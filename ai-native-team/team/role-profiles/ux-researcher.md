# UX Researcher (Part-Time / Contract)

**Reports to:** Senior Product Designer (dotted line to Product Manager)
**Function:** Design
**Engagement:** 0.4-0.6 FTE, typically contract or fractional

## Core Responsibilities
- Run customer interviews (10-15 per quarter) and usability tests
- Synthesize themes across interviews, surveys, support tickets, and analytics
- Maintain a research repository the whole team can search
- Translate findings into actionable design and product implications
- Own the customer-feedback loop: incoming signal → roadmap input

## AI-Augmented Responsibilities
- **AI handles:** transcribing and tagging interviews, clustering open-ended survey
  responses, summarizing support ticket trends, generating discussion guides from a
  research goal, drafting personas from quantitative signal
- **Human judges:** what people actually mean vs. what they said, which signals are
  noise, when to run more research vs. when to ship and learn, what a quote reveals
  about a deeper unmet need

## Key Skills to Hire For
- Strong qualitative interviewing — can hold space, follow threads, avoid leading
- Statistical literacy — knows when n=5 is enough and when it isn't
- Synthesis over volume — produces a 1-page insight memo, not a 40-slide deck
- Comfortable with AI transcription and clustering tools (Dovetail, Otter, Notably)
- Has experience embedded in a startup, not just agency or research-firm work

## Interview Signals (AI-native vs. traditional)
- **Strong:** Brings a one-page insight memo from a real study and can defend every
  claim with a quote
- **Strong:** Has a strong opinion on which AI synthesis tools are useful vs. which
  generate plausible-but-wrong clusters
- **Strong:** Can spot a leading question in their own discussion guide and rewrite it
- **Weak:** Defaults to surveys and dashboards; doesn't run live interviews
- **Weak:** Lets AI clustering output be the final insight without re-reading the source

## Compensation Band (US, fractional / contract)
- Rate: $150 - $225 per hour
- Or part-time salary equivalent: $90K - $130K for ~0.5 FTE

# Org Chart

An AI-native cross-functional team of 8-12 senior operators. Design, Engineering, and
Marketing operate as one unit — not three departments with handoffs. The Product Manager
is the connective tissue across all three.

```
                          Founder / CEO
                                │
                          Product Manager
                  (cross-functional, sits across all three)
                                │
         ┌──────────────────────┼──────────────────────┐
         │                      │                      │
      Design                Engineering              Marketing
       (2-3)                  (3-5)                   (2-3)
         │                      │                      │
  ┌──────┴──────┐    ┌──────────┼──────────┐    ┌─────┴──────┐
  │             │    │          │          │    │            │
  Senior      Visual/  Tech Lead   Mid-Sr.   DevOps  Strategic  Content/
  Product     Brand    Architect   Engineers  Infra  Marketer   Growth
  Designer    Designer            (2-3)              (lead)     Marketer
       │
   UX Researcher                                            (optional)
   (part-time/contract)                                     Data/Perf.
                                                            Marketer
```

## Design (2-3 people)
- **Senior Product Designer** — owns UX, rapid prototyping, accessibility. Uses AI for
  layout variations, user flow generation, and design audits.
- **Visual/Brand Designer** — owns brand identity, asset creation, illustration. Uses
  generative AI for exploration but brings human taste and judgment.
- **UX Researcher (part-time/contract)** — synthesizes interviews, surveys, and support
  tickets using AI. Focuses human hours on qualitative observation.

## Engineering (3-5 people)
- **Senior Architect / Tech Lead** — owns system design, code review, architecture
  decisions. Uses AI for documentation, trade-off analysis, and exploring approaches.
- **2-3 Mid-to-Senior Engineers** — ship features using AI as a pairing partner for
  code, tests, and debugging. Own judgment on structure and what to build.
- **Infrastructure / DevOps Engineer** — automates CI/CD, monitoring, incident
  response. Uses AI for log analysis, runbook generation, and deployment checklists.

## Marketing (2-3 people)
- **Strategic Marketer** — owns positioning, messaging, competitive intelligence.
  Uses AI for competitor analysis, positioning frameworks, and market signal synthesis.
- **Content / Growth Marketer** — produces high-volume quality content using AI.
  Brings editorial eye and brand sensibility. Acts as editor-in-chief of AI output.
- **Data / Performance Marketer (optional)** — owns campaign analysis, attribution,
  and reporting using AI-powered analytics.

## Cross-functional
- **Product Manager** — sits across all three functions. Uses AI for specs, research
  synthesis, and roadmap management.

## Hiring Order
1. Tech Lead — sets the engineering quality bar and AI-pairing culture
2. Strategic Marketer — establishes positioning before there's anything to launch
3. Senior Product Designer — sets the design system and quality bar
4. Mid-Senior Engineers (×2)
5. Content/Growth Marketer
6. Visual/Brand Designer
7. DevOps/Infra Engineer
8. Product Manager
9. UX Researcher (contract), 3rd engineer, Data Marketer — as scale demands
